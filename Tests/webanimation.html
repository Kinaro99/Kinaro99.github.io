Topic for this container: FRUITS!!!

What are fruits you ask. Merriam-Webster gives 4 definitions of fruits! I will list 3 as the last one is offensive!

  1. a product of plant growth (such as grain, vegetables, or cotton)
  2. offspring
  3. the state of bearing fruit also relating to the effect or consequence of an action or operation
For more information check out Merriam-Webster's definition of fruit!

I love fruits! If I could eat something for the rest of my life and never get tired of them.... I would eat fruits! Not the ones you find here in the States, but the ones found in most tropical countries. After moving here I came to realize how limited fruits are here which kinda sucks. Because I know you're dying to hear this, here's a some of my favourite fruits, in no particular order:)

  • Mangoes
  • Bananas
  • Papayas
  • Pineapples
  • Guava
  • Grapes --- only seeedlessss pleaseeee
  • Literally most fruits lol except avocadoes

Topic for this container: VEGETABLES!!!

What are vegetables you ask? Merriam-Webster says veggies are a usually herbaceous plant (such as the cabbage, bean, or potato) grown for an edible part that is usually eaten as part of a meal. For more information check out Merriam-Webster's definition of vegetable!

I'm not going to lie, I don't care for veggies as much as I care for fruits! I like most veggies raw because I like the crunchy taste of it all. I don't like boiled veggies but can handle steamed. The way I get veggies into my system are through smoothies or juices. I have a handy-dandy juicer and I literally juice anything and everything. If I can't juice it I make a smoothie or find some sort of interesting way to digest it lol.
